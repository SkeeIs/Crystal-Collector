Miner's Delight

Howdy 49er! This, here, quarry is your claim. You find a gem vein so big you should be able to get an ice block, a donkey, AND a dozen rasher of bacon in trade. You lucky So and So! Each type of gem in the vein has a unique & random value that is unknown to you. This vein isn't infinite though; if you over-mine it you go bankrupt & loose your claim! Clicking on the gems will mine them from the vein reducing the total amount of gems left. Good luck & be careful with that, there, dynamite!
